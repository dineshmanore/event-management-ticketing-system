Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect

We may collect personal identification information, including your name, email address, password hashes, and booking history when you voluntarily register and interact with the ShowTime platform.

2. How We Use Information

ShowTime relies on your data solely to secure your bookings, reserve your specific theater seats, verify account access, and communicate essential booking confirmation details directly to you.

3. Data Protection

Your secure data is never sold to third parties. Our servers utilize highly encrypted JSON Web Tokens (JWT), robust password hashing algorithms, and secure headers to fortify user safety.

4. Your Rights

You reserve complete autonomy over your data and may request an entire account deletion or contact support anytime with privacy concerns.
