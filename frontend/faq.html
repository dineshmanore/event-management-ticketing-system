Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book tickets?
Simply browse movies, select your preferred city, find a showtime, choose your seats, and complete the payment using our secure checkout flow.
Can I cancel my tickets?
Yes! If you head to your "My Bookings" dashboard, you can securely cancel your ticket. The seats will immediately become available again.
How do I get my M-ticket?
Your booking details will appear in your Dashboard. You can show that M-ticket page directly at the cinema or event entrance. No printed copies are required.
What secure payment methods do you support?
We accept credit/debit cards, UPI, Google Pay, Apple Pay, and digital wallets connected to the Razorpay network.
